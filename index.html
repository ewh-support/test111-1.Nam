December 1st update
Announcements
12/01/2019 by Novus

Greetings everyone!
It has been a wild year, but as snow starts to fall, that time of the year arrives where we wrap ourselves with the warmest of blankets, sit near the fire with those we love and drink some hot chocolate on this merry night.
December welcomes us with a new update with the changes/additions:
- Upgrades and fixes to the Client’s & Server’s performance.
- Addition of new tiles for the Christmas event maps and Unova.
- New cosmetics and mounts, including the winner of the PvE Shop Mount Vote, Metagross.
- Reworked the following Christmas forms: Munchlax-, Bunneary-, Teddiursa- and Riolu-family.
- Snow effect for the new and old Christmas forms in battles.
- Changed order in the battle bag. Balls are always shown first now.
- Christmas event part 1 is now live.
- Added Zangoose to Pinkan Island.

As for the PvP Season, the results are in and can be viewed here, congratulations to those who made it to the top 25!
If you want to see the update in detail or view past changes, you can head here.
